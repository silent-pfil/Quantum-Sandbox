# Quantum-Sandbox
Universe Sandbox at the quantum level
Hello everything and welcome to an idea I asked AI to help with.

I asked Claude to do something similar at the quantum scale.
I have no formal education or experience with physics.
Just curiosity and creativity. 
I'd love to hear constructive criticism, comments or anything else you have to say about this.

The real application will include the Tier 1 and 2 of this example as well as a Tier 3 and 4.
Tier 3 will be a Particle Collider Simulator.
Tier 4 will be the full Quantum Sandbox where you can place Hydrogen and Oxygen and watch water form.
